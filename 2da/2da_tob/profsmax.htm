profsmax.2da

This file defines how many proficiencies * a character will have at his/her creation, and how often he/she will get an other one.
First column is the list of classes identifiers
2nd column is just the maximal number of proficiency * given at the PC creation from the class defined in column 1st.
3rd column is the maximal amount of proficiency * that could be put in weapon proficiencies on other levels.
