ABRACERQ.2DA

This file defines minimum and maximum statistics per race.

The first row (titles of columns ) is filled by the names of defined characteristics of PC (like MIN_STR = minimum strength).

The first column is filled by race names.

The crossings of the certain identifiers indicate what are intervals for statistics of PC with chosen race - used for character creation.
