--- n: 55 name: "Death: Kill Creature Type" param1: "IDS Entry" param2: "IDS File" bg1: 1 bg2: 1 bgee: 1 iwd1: 1 iwd2: 1 pst: 0 --- Kills the targeted creature(s) with HD less than those specified by the Dice values, if the target(s) match the IDS mask.
Known values for 'IDS File' are:
2 EA.ids / EnemyAlly.ids (in IWDs)
3 General.ids
4 Race.ids
5 Class.ids
6 Specific.ids
7 Gender.ids
8 Align.ids
9 Kit.ids (EE-only)
'IDS Entry' is a value from the specified IDS file, indicating the specific creature type to affect.
