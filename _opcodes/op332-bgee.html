--- n: 332 name: "Stat: Specific Damage Modifier" param1: "Modifier" param2: "Type" bg1: 0 bg2: 0 bgee: 1 iwd1: 0 iwd2: 0 pst: 0 --- Increases the damage caused by the character. The 'Type' parameter defines which damage type is increased. The modifier is either a flat value modifier for type 0, or a percentile modifier for all other cases.
Known values for 'Type' are:
0 all damage increased
1 Fire damage
2 Cold damage
3 Electricity damage
4 Acid damage
5 Magic damage
6 Poison damage
7 Slashing damage
8 Piercing damage
9 Crushing damage
10 Missile damage
