Known values for 'Type' are:
0 Mage Spells
1 Cleric Spells
2 Innate
3 Mage Spells
4 Cleric Spells
5 Innate
'Type' values of 0,1 or 2 display a message -'Spell Failed: casting failure'
'Type' values of 3,4 or 5 display a message -'Spell failed due to dead magic'
