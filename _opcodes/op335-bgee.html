Known values for 'Spellstate' are defined in SPLSTATE.IDS.
'Row#' selects the blocked spell or effect, the blocking criteria are listed in 7EYES.2DA thus works like a very selective spell trap.
'Eye Group' may be used to define separate eye groups (it will remove 336 opcodes of the same type).
