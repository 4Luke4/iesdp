--- n: 335 name: "Spell Effect: Seven Eyes" param1: "State" param2: "Type" special: "Eye Group" bg1: 0 bg2: 0 bgee: 1 iwd1: 0 iwd2: 0 pst: 0 --- This effect sets spellstate 'State', increases the seven eyes stat and grants special ability named in the resource field.
'Type' selects the blocked spell or effect, the blocking criteria are listed in 7eyes.2da thus works like a very selective spell trap.
'Eye Group' may be used to define separate eye groups (it will remove 336 opcodes of the same type).
