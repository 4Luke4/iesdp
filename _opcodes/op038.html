--- n: 38 name: "State: Silence" param1: "Irrelevant" param2: "Irrelevant" bg1: 1 bg2: 1 bgee: 1 iwd1: 1 iwd2: 1 pst: 1 --- Applies the silence effect to the targeted creature(s). A silenced creature cannot cast spells, use special abilities or initiate dialog. The engine is hard-coded to allow these spells to be cast by a silenced creature:
  • SPWI219 (Vocalize)
  • SPIN649 (Teleport to Pocket Plane) in BG2/BGEE
