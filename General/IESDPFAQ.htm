  • What is the IESDP ?   


    This is Infinity Engine Structures Description Project - it is intended to research and interpret various data about Infinity Engine (r) produced by BioWare Corp. 

     
    Top


  • What are the IESDP goals ? 

    To provide all possible information and assistance for add-on /add-in/ Total Conversions authors/developers and editors that use Infinity Engine as the base.

    To provide information and assistance for tools programmers.

    To research requested information.

  • What are techniques of research ?

    There are three of them that I may list straight (if you know any more let me know :) ) :

    1) Empirical research - we change something in analyzed structure and we check how it has changed situation in IE game.

    2) Logical analysis - we consider some amount of examples of some structure (for example 10 files of the same type but describing some different objects , like 10 different areas files). Then we focus on some structures inside them finding similarities and differences drawing conclusions about structure and supposed functions from logical argumentation, finding general rule for some effects.

    3) Analyzing source code of IE engine - not available since source code is not available for our research.
    Top


  • What tools are used in research ?

    Mainly these may be limited to the following : IE games, Hex Editor, Infinity Explorer, Near Infinity and WinBiff - these are basic tools - all other tools may be used to obtain additional information , like experiments with unknown fields in IEEP when editing spells files for example.
