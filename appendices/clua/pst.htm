Activating the Console
Enable
The **cheats were disabled** in Planescape and can easily crash the game. You can reenable them by installing [Bubb's enabler mod](https://github.com/Bubb13/IE-Snippets/tree/master/bubb_enable_pst_console).
Access
Press 'L' to activate the cheat panel.


Cheat Keys
Press 'L' to activate the cheat panel.

Using the Console
Commands
shazam("<string>")
Move the selected characters to the area specified by <string>.
